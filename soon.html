Let us discover together this awesome car and understand what makes it so fabulous and unique.



With only a weight of around a 1000 kilograms the pagani zonda seats at a top speed of 375 km/h with an acceleration from 0 to 100km/h in 2.8 seconds. No more being late in a car like this.

Under the hood this luxurious supercar hides a powerful V12 engine from the german tuner AMG.

It is powered by a 5,987 cc V12 naturally aspirated petrol engine supplied by Mercedes-Benz from the S-Class W140, which develops 550hp.

The overall body of the car is covered in carbon fiber which makes it more resistant to shocks and improves safety. It also increase speed due to it's light weight.
